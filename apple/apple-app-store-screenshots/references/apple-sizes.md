# Apple App Store Connect screenshot sizes

Source: https://developer.apple.com/help/app-store-connect/reference/app-information/screenshot-specifications

All sizes are in pixels. Files must be PNG or JPG, RGB color, no alpha channel. Apple rejects uploads that do not match one of these exact dimensions.

## iPhone

### 6.9" displays (iPhone 17 Pro Max, 17 Pro, Air, 16 Pro Max, 16 Pro, 15 Pro Max)
- 1320 × 2868 (portrait)
- 2868 × 1320 (landscape)

### 6.7" / 6.5" displays (iPhone 16 Plus, 15 Plus, 14 Pro Max, 14 Plus, 13 Pro Max, 12 Pro Max, 11 Pro Max, XS Max, 11, XR)
- 1290 × 2796 (portrait, 6.7")
- 2796 × 1290 (landscape, 6.7")
- 1284 × 2778 (portrait, 6.5")
- 2778 × 1284 (landscape, 6.5")
- 1242 × 2688 (portrait, 6.5")
- 2688 × 1242 (landscape, 6.5")

### 6.1" / 6.3" displays (iPhone 16, 15, 14 Pro, 13 Pro, 13, 12 Pro, 12, 11 Pro, XS, X)
- 1206 × 2622 (portrait)
- 2622 × 1206 (landscape)
- 1179 × 2556 (portrait)
- 2556 × 1179 (landscape)

### 5.5" displays (iPhone 8 Plus, 7 Plus, 6s Plus)
- 1242 × 2208 (portrait)
- 2208 × 1242 (landscape)

### 4.7" displays (iPhone SE 2nd/3rd gen, 8, 7, 6s)
- 750 × 1334 (portrait)
- 1334 × 750 (landscape)

### 4" displays (iPhone SE 1st gen)
- 640 × 1096 (portrait without status bar)
- 640 × 1136 (portrait)
- 1136 × 600 (landscape without status bar)
- 1136 × 640 (landscape)

- 768 × 1004 (portrait without status bar)

## Mac

One of the following, 16:10 aspect ratio:
- 1280 × 800
- 1440 × 900
- 2560 × 1600
- 2880 × 1800
